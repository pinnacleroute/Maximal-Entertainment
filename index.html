About the Studio

Six Decades of Combined Filmmaking Experience

Maximal Entertainment, LLC — formerly known as NiterBoys Entertainment, LLC — is a film and entertainment production company based in South Florida. The team brings together more than sixty years of combined experience across film and music, including film school graduates, cinematographers, an acting coach, actors, graphic artists, musicians, and music producers.

To date, the company has produced numerous music videos, commercials, and short and feature films — working alongside partner production companies to bring stories to the screen, with several feature films already complete and additional original projects currently in development.
